Ritapa Neogi is an artist born and raised in Portland, Oregon.

She has illustrated for Portland Parks & Recreation, the City of Vancouver, Black Girl Dangerous, Vision Zero Vancouver, and publishers in Kolkata, India. Ritapa holds a B.A. in Geography & Urban Studies and a Master of Community & Regional Planning. Her career as a sustainability professional in Vancouver, British Columbia facilitates her understanding of creative scientific communications for stakeholders and the public.

In her free time, Ritapa enjoys writing music, swimming in the ocean, biking around Vancouver, and participating in the city's Spanish-English language exchange community.
